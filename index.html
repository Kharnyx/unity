Kharnyx is an indie developer offering a collection of high-quality 3D models for Unity. Their goal is to provide affordable assets that don't skimp on quality, perfect for fellow indie developers and hobbyists looking to make their projects stand out.

Kharnyx creates versatile 3D models that can fit into a wide range of game styles. Whether developers are building a fantasy world or crafting a futuristic sci-fi universe, they will find something useful in the Kharnyx store. There is no specific theme for these models—they are designed to be flexible and easy to use in whatever game world developers are bringing to life.

Kharnyx keeps costs low because they believe in making great assets accessible to everyone. They encourage developers to check back regularly for new additions, and to reach out if they need anything. Kharnyx is always happy to help!
