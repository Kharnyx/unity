Home

Hi, I'm Kharnyx, a freelance developer offering a collection of high-quality 3D models for Unity. My goal is to provide affordable assets that don’t compromise on quality, perfect for fellow freelance developers and hobbyists looking to make their projects stand out.

I create versatile 3D models that can fit into a wide range of game styles. Whether you’re building a fantasy world or crafting a futuristic sci-fi universe, you’ll find something useful in my store. There’s no specific theme for these models—they’re designed to be flexible and easy to use in whatever game world you’re creating.

I keep costs low because I believe in making great assets accessible to everyone. I encourage developers to check back regularly for new additions and to reach out if they need anything. I’m always happy to help!
